About Me

Researcher & Innovation Catalyst

I am a dedicated researcher with a passion for understanding how collective intelligence can be enhanced through artificial intelligence while addressing the inherent biases in group decision-making processes. My work bridges the gap between human cognition, technology, and social dynamics.

With a background spanning from agricultural sciences to collective intelligence, I bring a unique interdisciplinary perspective to complex research challenges. My journey from Nigeria to Morocco, and now to Ireland, has shaped my understanding of cross-cultural collaboration and diverse thinking patterns.

I am currently pursuing my PhD research at TU Dublin, investigating how AI can facilitate better collective decision-making. Previously, I served as an Outreach Officer at UM6P's Africa Initiative, where I facilitated sustainable development initiatives across Africa.
